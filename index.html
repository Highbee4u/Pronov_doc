Welcome

Information is essential to management. Collecting, processing and disseminating information may seems costly,

but the cost of developing and maintaining an information system should be balanced

the cost of ineffective inventory control. The goals of ProNov App are to protect store items from loss, damages, theft or wastage and to manage the reliable movement of supplies from pharmaceutical Manufacturing company to the Pharmaceutical stock keeper in most economical and expeditious way.

The objective of ProNov App is to make inventory decisions that minimize the total cost of inventory. This is not to be confused with minimizing inventory.
