Heading

ProNOV is a lean mobile-based business intelligent solution that helps micro-retailers manage their inventory, ensure on-time availability of goods, help them source goods cheaper through bulk breaking, and potentially pass on the lower cost of goods to the targeted population in the Bottom of Pyramid (BoP). For this roll-out phase, ProNOV is deployed at the grassroot micro-pharmacies to improve the availability and affordability of critical life saving drugs while eliminating counterfeiting.
